    Drake Invaders

    Open README

    A 'Space Invaders' style game where the user plays as rapper Drake trying to shoot down the enemy (in this case DJ Khaled) with stacks of cash. The player starts with three lives and loses one each time they get hit by a falling key. The aim of the game is to clear the board of enemies.

    Who Dat?

    Open README

    A multiple choice music quiz which plays a 30 second snippet of music. The player must guess the artist from four options. There are 25 questions in each category and nine categories to choose from.
